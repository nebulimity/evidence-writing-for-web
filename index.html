Writing for the Web

Koen Kruk | Web & App Development | Career Tech

Quick Tips:

  • Content should be written in an easily readable style.
  • Put the most important content in the first paragraph.
  • Cover only one topic per paragraph.

Write meaningful headings

Writing meaningful headings makes it easier for users to navigate your page. If what they're looking for is furthur down the page, they can scroll and find the right paragraph at a glance.

Keep it short

Writing for the web needs to be much shorter than other kinds of writing. A paragraph should be 70 words or fewer.

Use a readable font

Using a readable font like Serif improves reading speed due to the style of the text.

Maintain proper background to text contrast

If the text is too hard to read or the background is too bright, it can cause fatigue on the eyes
